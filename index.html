SCHOOL OF RIGHTEOUSNESS: SPIRIT, SOUL & BODY

We are a church that believes in Jesus, a church that loves God and people. We have a strong sense of community with parishioners. people and children of all ages here are encouraged to learn about their own faith and the role of the church in our community and worldwide.
